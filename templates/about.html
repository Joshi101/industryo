About CoreLogs

CoreLogs is one-stop Business Enabling platform for SMEs giving all SMEs a uniform identity on Internet, helping them connect with each other and providing a basic CRM (Customer Relationship Management) services.)

What you can do on CoreLogs ?

Simply put, a lot.

Workplace Profile is meant to replace the need of a personal website for each company providing a more efficient way to fullfil the same need.

The Marketplace is an agglomeration of products sold by the small and medium scale enterpises to another business or individual. List your product or find one, its a hassle free service.

The Feed is an attempt to bring the huge number of Engineering Minds in the country to a common platform, discussing, exploring and solving problems in a new yet powerful way. Q&A, Articles and the latest updates everything is there.

And above all its the People we don't want to loose our focus on. A company is nothing without the people behind it. You can always find people you need in your professional life.

What's already there at CoreLogs ?

More than 3000 users from over 1000 industries, institutes and engineering teams are already active. Industries accross the country have listed over 1000 Products accross 300 + Categories on the Marketplace and curious minds are bringing new questions and their solutions at the Engineer's Forum.

CoreLogs is under developement at the time and selected companies and students are invited as a part of the beta phase. Feel free to try out the platform for yourself and your company.

Where are we heading ?

Being a one stop platform for SMEs, the manufacturing industries. You want to find different manufacturers & suppliers of a product or a product category, companies which have setup & machinery to perform particular operation, get to know about a company, the people in it, get connected to people in it, place order directly or anything, you come to CoreLogs.

You want to get connected to the whole SME community update it about something, you want to discuss some technical or industry related problem with this community, you come to CoreLogs.

And of course, you own or work for an SME, you want to list products, get a seller's backend, handle product inquiries and orders seamlessly get a company profile for your SME where all industries are there already, you want your company to appear in searches based on operations, machinery etc, you come to CoreLogs.

In short, we are heading to become an entire Social Network of SMEs.

Who is telling you all this ?

CoreLogs is a Startup headquartered in Bangalore. We are a small team of passionate engineers graduated from IITs & NITs. We have spent a long time being part of an Automobile team, visiting SMEs, getting help & helping them, exploring the challenges we seek to solve here. We have seen Industries inside out and are no strangers to the conditions prevailing in the segement in our country.
